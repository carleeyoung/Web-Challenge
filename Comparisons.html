Comparisons

Latitude Vs Temperature Correlation

Temperature and latitude are directly proportional when moving north from the southern hemisphere toward the equator. As the latitude increases, the temperature also increases. When moving south from the northern hemisphere toward the equator, temperature and latitude are inversely proportional. As the latitude decreases, the temperature increases.

Latitude Vs Humidity Correlation

Humidity and latitude show no significant correlation. As you move closer to or further from the equator, humidity does not change in proportion to the latitude.

Latitude Vs Cloudiness Correlation

Cloudiness and latitude show no significant correlation. As you move closer to or further from the equator, cloudiness does not change in proportion to the latitude.

Latitude Vs Wind Speed Correlation

Wind Speed and latitudeshow no significant correlation. As you move closer to or further from the equator, cloudiness does not change in proportion to the latitude.
